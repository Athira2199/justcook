TOMATO BASIL SANDWICH

A simple and delicious sandwich recipe made with tomatoes and the flavourful basil

INGREDIENTS TO BE USED
3 medium tomatoes, washed
15-20 basil leaves
4 slices of brown bread
4 slices of white bread
Salt to taste
A pinch of black pepper powder
1 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
Butter for applying
Cheese spread for applying
Tomato ketchup to serve

VEG MAYO SANDWICH

A simple sandwich with mayonnaise and vegetable stuffing.

INGREDIENTS TO BE USED
8 white bread slices
1 cup fresh cream
150 ml oil
Salt to taste
½ tsp sugar
1 tsp mustard powder
1 medium yellow bell pepper, seeded and thinly sliced
1 medium carrot, peeled and thinly sliced
1 medium red capsicum, seeded and thinly sliced
¼ medium cabbage, thinly sliced
¼ medium red cabbage, thinly sliced
Butter to apply
2 cheese slices
Green chutney for serving
French fries for serving
