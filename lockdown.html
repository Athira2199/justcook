CHEESE BURST SPRING ROLLS

Gooey cheese spiced with dried red chilli flakes, mixed with bell peppers and stuffed between wonton sheets and deep fried to golden gorgeousness.

INGREDIENTS TO BE USED
100 grams processed cheese block
100 grams mozzarella cheese block
6-8 spring roll sheets
Sauce
1 tsp butter
1 tsp refined flour (maida)
¾ cup milk
Salt to taste
Stuffing
2-3 green chillies, chopped
½ tsp dried red chilli flakes
1 small red capsicum, chopped
1 small green capsicum, chopped
1 small yellow capsicum, chopped
Salt to taste
Frying
Refined flour slurry as required
Oil for deep frying
To serve
Sweet chilli sauce

VEG MAYO SANDWICH

A simple sandwich with mayonnaise and vegetable stuffing.

INGREDIENTS TO BE USED
8 white bread slices
1 cup fresh cream
150 ml oil
Salt to taste
½ tsp sugar
1 tsp mustard powder
1 medium yellow bell pepper, seeded and thinly sliced
1 medium carrot, peeled and thinly sliced
1 medium red capsicum, seeded and thinly sliced
¼ medium cabbage, thinly sliced
¼ medium red cabbage, thinly sliced
Butter to apply
2 cheese slices
Green chutney for serving
French fries for serving

TOMATO BASIL SANDWICH

A simple and delicious sandwich recipe made with tomatoes and the flavourful basil

INGREDIENTS TO BE USED
3 medium tomatoes, washed
15-20 basil leaves
4 slices of brown bread
4 slices of white bread
Salt to taste
A pinch of black pepper powder
1 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
Butter for applying
Cheese spread for applying
Tomato ketchup to serve
